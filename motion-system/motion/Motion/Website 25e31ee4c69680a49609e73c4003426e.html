Types

Types of Motion in our design system include Animations and Interactions. These types work together to create a dynamic, engaging, and intuitive user experience by providing visual feedback, enhancing usability, and guiding user interactions.

Animations

On our website, animations are visual effects triggered by events like loading a page or scrolling. They make elements like text, images, or buttons move or change smoothly, such as sliding menus or fading text.

Interactions

Interactions occur when you perform actions like clicking a button or hovering over an item. `These actions prompt our website to respond, providing feedback or changing elements based on what you do.

Styles

Our styles feature smooth transitions, consistent timing, and fluid motion. These enhance the user experience by combining elegance with functionality, ensuring a cohesive and engaging interface with visual harmony across animations and interactions.

Elements

Elements integrate seamlessly to enhance the user experience, providing visual feedback and guiding interactions. Together, they ensure a cohesive, dynamic, and intuitive interface that maintains consistency and engagement throughout the website.
