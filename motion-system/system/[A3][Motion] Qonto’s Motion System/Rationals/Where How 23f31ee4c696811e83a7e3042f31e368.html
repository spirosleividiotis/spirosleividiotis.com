Where/How

Context

This page captures the thinking behind how we defined the purpose and possible use of each motion value in our system.

It doesn’t assign behavior to components or serve as handoff documentation. Instead, it answers:

  • Why does duration-100 exist?
  • Where could scale-95 make sense?
  • What type of interaction is easing-emphasized suited for?

It helps designers and developers understand the intent behind each token, so they can make better choices when building flows, screens, and interactions — even before we define exact component behaviors.

This is part of how we bring clarity and consistency to motion in the early stages of our design system.
